# Copy Analysis & Suggestions: learnslice.com
**Date:** 2026-04-04 (Re-scored after implementation)
**Page Type:** Homepage + /companies landing page
**Copy Score:** 81/100 (up from 68/100)

---

## Executive Summary

After a full round of copy improvements, LearnSlice's messaging is significantly stronger. The dual-problem headline ("Azubis ohne Plan. Ausbilder am Limit.") captures both sides of the pain and creates emotional urgency. The full-cycle differentiator (IHK prep + onboarding + upskilling + company knowledge) now runs through every touchpoint — separating LearnSlice from IHK-only competitors. Numbers are consistent with the calculator (€15K/apprentice), CTAs are unified, and FAQ answers now sell.

**What improved:**
- Headline now names both audiences' pain (was single-sided)
- Subheadline communicates the full-cycle differentiator with savings
- All savings numbers aligned to calculator (was contradictory: €4K vs €32K)
- CTAs unified to "Book My Free Demo" / "Kostenlose Demo buchen" (was 6 variants)
- FAQ answers end with benefits and soft CTAs (were purely informational)
- Vocational Professions moved higher on page (was buried at #9)
- Meta descriptions match the new headline angle

**Remaining opportunities:**
- Testimonials still describe the tool, not outcomes (requires customer outreach)
- No dedicated "Problem" section before "How It Works" (emotional gap)
- "AI Performance Catalyst" remains unexplained jargon

## Value Proposition Analysis

```
TARGET CUSTOMER:  German companies, schools, and agencies with apprentices
                  (Mittelstand HR leads, Geschäftsführung, Ausbildungsleiter)
PROBLEM:          Azubis without structure (can't prep for IHK, struggle to
                  integrate, drop out at 30%). Ausbilder overwhelmed (12 hrs/week
                  per apprentice, burning out).
SOLUTION:         Full-cycle AI platform: IHK exam prep + onboarding + upskilling
                  + company knowledge integration
UNIQUE MECHANISM: Full training cycle in one platform (competitors only do IHK prep).
                  AI adapts 24/7 to each apprentice's gaps.
KEY BENEFIT:      Apprentices deliver value in weeks, not months. Trainers get 50%
                  of their time back. Up to €15K saved per apprentice.
PROOF:            30+ companies, BMWE AI Prize 2025, FH Dortmund partnership,
                  3 named testimonials, calculator-backed ROI
```

**Remaining gaps:**
- **Testimonials lack outcomes.** Still the single highest-impact improvement available. Requires customer outreach to get specific numbers.
- **No dedicated problem section.** The headline names the pain, but the page jumps to logos → ROI → How It Works. A 2-3 sentence problem amplification section between logos and ROI would deepen the emotional hook.
- **"AI Performance Catalyst" unexplained.** The features section badge is jargon. Should say something like "AI That Learns Your Apprentices" or "KI, die Ihre Azubis kennt."
