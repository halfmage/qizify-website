# Qualification: inbound leads from /de/weiterbildungsanbieter

Use on every inbound (Strategiegespräch request, lead-magnet download). Goal: spend bespoke-build effort only where need AND a funding path coincide. Gate hard on scale.

## Fast disqualifiers
- Single-site micro provider with no IT role and no external-software history → route to a future productized/self-serve offer, not a custom build.
- Pure tender-bound public body with no framework and a first-deal expectation of <3 months → nurture, do not invest yet.

## BANT
- **Budget:** Is there capex (corporate/tuition/foundation), or do we package as per-seat opex (Sachkosten) + ESF+? If neither, deprioritize.
- **Authority:** Who is in the room (champion vs. QMB vs. IT/Datenschutz vs. Geschäftsführung)? Map the committee; identify the economic buyer early.
- **Need:** Which trigger is active, BFSG, AZAV 1 Jul 2025 rule, SGB IX accessibility, AI initiative, tender win, incumbent-tool pain? No active trigger → lower priority.
- **Timeline:** Tied to a recertification window, tender date, or funding deadline? Concrete dates raise priority.

## MEDDIC (for larger deals)
- **Metrics:** completion/Verbleibsquote uplift, documentation/audit effort saved, accessibility risk removed, cohort-onboarding speed.
- **Economic buyer:** Geschäftsführung / Kaufmännische Leitung confirmed.
- **Decision criteria:** DSGVO + DE/EU hosting, AZAV documentation fit, BFSG accessibility, Moodle/ILIAS integration, ownership of data.
- **Decision process:** committee path + any Vergabe/tender requirement; EVB-IT/DPA if public.
- **Identify pain:** the specific regulation or operational gap forcing action.
- **Champion:** E-Learning-Verantwortliche or Pädagogische Leitung who will carry it internally.

## Scoring → next step
- **A (≥ Tier 1, active trigger, funding path, champion):** book Strategiegespräch, prep with `../sales-prep` framing.
- **B (need but unclear funding or authority):** send lead magnet + relevant case study, nurture, re-qualify on trigger.
- **C (micro/no budget/tender-only):** add to product/self-serve waitlist; no bespoke effort.
