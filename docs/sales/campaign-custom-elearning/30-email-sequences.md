# Cold email sequences (targeted, low-volume)

Formal Sie. No em or en dashes. Never name a competitor. Positioning is custom project + optional
managed run.

---

## §7 UWG risk box (read before any send)

Unsolicited B2B cold email in Germany requires prior consent. There is no safe statutory basis
for pure prospecting email, so every send here carries Abmahnung and fine risk. The owner has
decided to proceed with targeted email anyway. Non-negotiable guardrails:

- **Personalize to one documented mandate.** If the `[konkreter Bezug]` token is generic, do not
  send. One relevant reason per recipient.
- **Impressum + one-click opt-out** in every email (footer below).
- **One follow-up maximum**, then stop. No third touch to a non-responder.
- **Suppression list.** An opt-out is permanent and applies across all channels.
- **Low daily volume.** This is both the legal and the deliverability guardrail.
- **Public sector: email is the exception,** used only for a published Referat business contact
  about a specific live mandate. Prefer LinkedIn and tender-monitoring for that segment.

---

## Deliverability setup (do this first, or the campaign fails silently)

- Send from a **separate sending domain** (for example a `.de` variant), not the primary
  learnslice.com domain, so any reputation hit does not damage the main domain.
- Configure **SPF, DKIM, and DMARC** on the sending domain.
- **Warm up** the domain gradually over two to three weeks before real outreach.
- Keep **daily volume low** and ramp slowly. Low volume serves both deliverability and §7.
- Monitor bounces and spam complaints; pause immediately on trouble.
- Send as a real person from a real mailbox, plain text, no tracking-heavy templates.

## Value-asset dependency

These emails point to the live, self-serve **Digital-Readiness-Check**
(`learnslice.com/de/digital-readiness-check`): a 3-minute interactive self-assessment that gives
the prospect an instant, personalized Maßnahmenplan and captures their answers as a qualified
lead. Link to it rather than promising an attachment. Append `?src=email` for channel attribution.
The evidence brief (learnslice.com/research or the one-page leave-behind) is the fallback.

## Footer (paste into every email)

> LearnSlice, [Firmenname und Rechtsform], [Anschrift]. Vollständiges Impressum:
> learnslice.com/impressum. Wenn Sie keine weiteren Nachrichten von uns wünschen, antworten Sie
