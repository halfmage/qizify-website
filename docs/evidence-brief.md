## The credibility trio (top band)

- **University co-development:** Built with the IDiAL institute at FH Dortmund.
- **Federally funded research:** Funded by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Affairs and Energy (BMWE), grant 16GM200302.
- **Hosted in Germany:** Learner data stays in Germany and is never used to train the model.

---

## What the first pilot showed

- **100% of answers were traceable to a source.** Every answer carried a valid citation back to the source passage, with no invented references.
- **40 of 50 students reached near-complete mastery** of the exam topic after preparing with LearnSlice.
- **35 of 50 students found the learning pace appropriate** for their individual needs.
- **Rated higher than general AI tools** for exam-oriented and structured learning guidance.

## What it means for your role

**For leaders.**
A credible, compliant choice. Co-developed with a German university in a federally funded
project, with learner data hosted in Germany and never used to train the model. Predictable
fixed-cost hosting, not metered per-token billing.

**For teachers.**
It answers from your curriculum, and every statement links back to the source passage. You
keep control of the content, with no invented references. Designed with experienced lecturers
to support teaching, not replace it.

**For HR and L&D.**
Built for the skills gap in vocational training. You can feed it your own and regulated
material, with a roadmap toward company-specific knowledge. The university experts noted the
value is highest exactly where L&D operates: specialized, regulated, and curated-source domains.

## How it differs from general AI tools

| | LearnSlice | General AI tools |
|---|---|---|
| Data-processing location | Hosted in Germany | Vendor cloud, region varies, often outside the EU |
| Learner data used for training | No | Governed by vendor terms, varies by plan |
| Primary knowledge source | Your curriculum and documents | General model knowledge |
| Source attribution | Inline citations to retrieved passages | Not provided by default |
| Cost model | Fixed self-hosted infrastructure | Usage-based per-token pricing |
