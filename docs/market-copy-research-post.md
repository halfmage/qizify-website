## Voice profile (match existing brand)
- Formality 4/5 (Sie-Form, professional), Emotion 2/5 (sober), Complexity 3/5 (concrete, not
  academic), Humor 1/5 (serious), Authority 4/5 (expert but not arrogant).
- Principle: claims are earned with numbers and sources. Lead confidently from the proof; no
  hedging. No hype words ("revolutionaer", "game-changer").

## Value proposition (for this post)
- Target: Ausbilder / Ausbildungsleiter weighing a KI-Lernplattform, plus duale Studierende
  facing the IHK exam under Doppelbelastung.
- Problem: under double time pressure, unsure whether KI im Lernen real results brings or just
  costs extra time and risks wrong, unsourced answers.
- Proof: independent university pilot (FH Dortmund), not a vendor claim.
- Key benefit framing: "results you can check", not "magic gains".
