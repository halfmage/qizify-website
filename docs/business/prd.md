# LearnSlice Website - Business Product Requirements Document

## Product Overview

LearnSlice is an AI-powered learning platform focused on automating mentoring and training for apprenticeships and early-career talent. The new website will serve as the primary marketing and conversion tool, emphasizing the AI tutor agent capabilities and demonstrating clear ROI to potential customers.

**Target URLs**: 
- English (default): https://learnslice.com/
- German: https://learnslice.com/de/

## Target Audience

### Primary Personas

1. **CEOs** (Small to Medium Enterprises)
   - Decision-makers seeking cost reduction
   - Focus on ROI and business outcomes
   - Concerned about apprentice retention and productivity

2. **HR Professionals**
   - Responsible for training programs
   - Managing trainer workload
   - Seeking scalable training solutions

3. **Trainers**
   - Directly involved in apprentice training
   - Overwhelmed by repetitive instruction
   - Want to focus on high-value mentoring

### Company Profile
- German companies (SMEs)
- Companies with apprenticeship programs (Azubis)
- Organizations in IT Services, Manufacturing, Insurance, Healthcare, Government sectors
- Companies seeking to reduce training costs and improve retention

## Key Value Propositions

### Primary Value Proposition
**€32K–€41K saved per young talent. Big savings, quick ROI.**

### Core Benefits

1. **Speed to Productivity**
   - 65% faster time-to-productivity (4-6 months → 8 weeks)
   - New hires work on real, job-critical tasks from day one

2. **Cost Reduction**
   - 50% less trainer time = €10K saved per apprentice
   - Up to 50% less training effort for senior teams
   - €4K saved per apprentice, €20K per 5 apprentices

3. **Retention Improvement**
   - Dropout reduced: 30% → 15%
   - Up to 15% fewer dropouts during onboarding and apprenticeship
   - Personalized learning paths keep apprentices on track

4. **AI-Powered Mentoring**
   - 24/7 AI coaching and mentoring
   - AI mentor handles standard knowledge and daily practice
   - Experts focus on value creation, not basic instruction

5. **Compliance & Quality**
   - Built-in IHK learning path (prebuilt curriculum mapped to regional IHK requirements)
   - 100% GDPR-compliant
   - Hosted in Germany or your infrastructure
   - Science-proven learning methods

## Success Metrics

### Primary KPIs

1. **Conversion Rate**
   - Demo request form submissions
   - Target: 2-5% conversion rate from visitors to demo requests

2. **Engagement Metrics**
   - Time on page (target: 3+ minutes)
   - Scroll depth (target: 70%+ reach bottom)
   - Section interaction (slider usage, language route switching)

3. **Traffic Metrics**
   - Organic search traffic
   - Referral traffic from partner sites
   - Direct traffic

4. **Business Metrics**
   - Qualified leads generated
   - Demo-to-customer conversion rate
   - Cost per acquisition

### Secondary KPIs

- Bounce rate (target: <50%)
- Pages per session
- Return visitor rate
- Language preference (EN vs DE)
